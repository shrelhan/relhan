Our Story

The Relhan Legacy: From Dhaba to top Quality Sweets

The Foundation (1960s) In the early 1960s, our journey began with Gurbaksh Lal Ji and the iconic Relhan Dhaba. Known affectionately by the community as "Gurdayal Ji Hotal Wale" the dhaba became a staple of honest, soul-satisfying food. For two decades, it served as a gathering place where quality was never compromised and every guest was treated like family.

The Evolution (The Sweet Resolution) With twenty years of success in the restaurant world, Gurbaksh Lal Ji pivoted to a new craft, founding Relhan Sweets Corner. He did so with a singular, unwavering resolution: to create sweets using only the finest raw materials. He believed that purity wasn't just a goal, it was a responsibility to the people who trusted the Relhan name. We still source the branded ingredients, just as Gurbaksh Lal Ji did in the 60s. But now, we are pairing that old-world integrity with a new-world vision, ensuring that the Relhan Sweets legacy continues to thrive in a modern era.
"Same Purity. New Generation. Since 1960."
